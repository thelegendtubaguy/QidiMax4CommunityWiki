# FAQ

**Q: Does the Max 4 toolhead hit a wall when moving away from the waste chute like the Q2?**

**A:** No, the design of the printer avoids this entirely.
![Waste Chute Parking](/assets/waste_chute_parking.jpg)

**Q: It doesn't seem like OTA updates are working on my new Max 4, why?**

**A:** See [this page](./faq/initial_offline_firmware_update.md).

**Q: Why don't I see my bed mesh in Fluidd?**

**A:** You need to check "Enable Full Display" in your Fluidd settings.
![Fluidd Full Display](/assets/fluidd_enable_full_display.png)

**Q: I've messed with my Klipper configs and now I want to go back to stock, how do I do this?**

**A:** You can reference the stock configurations at [this repository](https://github.com/thelegendtubaguy/Qidi-Max-4-Config-Files/tree/main)
